What is our Mission

Solar revolution aims to provide the world's entire energy needs through solar. We collaborate with volunteers and sponsors to leverage our barganing power in the government to encourage policies which support green and rewenable energy such as solar and wind. We rely heavy on our volunteers to help in our projects and we team up with emploees who hire our dedicated volunteers to be a part of their team
